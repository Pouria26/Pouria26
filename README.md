## 👋 About Me

I'm fascinated by a simple question:

> How can machines understand and work with human language?

That question led me to explore Artificial Intelligence, particularly Large Language Models (LLMs), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Agentic AI systems.

I use this space to learn in public, build projects, experiment with ideas, and document my progress as I continue exploring AI.

**There's still a lot I don't know — and that's exactly what keeps me curious.**
